A five-year-old hospital with a capacity of 113 beds consisting of nine floors with a total area of 9000 square meters.
The hospital has 14 basic specialties including the 24 hour emergency department, as well as the hair and beauty section.
The hospital started work five years ago with continuous development in terms of physical income, medical professional development and integrated health services
The medical staff of the hospital consists of 54 doctors and more than 100 nurses, in addition to other specialties
The total number of employees is about 350 employees.
The hospital was accredited by the standards and standards of Turkish hospitals by 98%
Medical and Quality Management for the year. He also holds the ISO 9000 International Quality Certificate and the Turkish Standards and Metrology Center Certificate of Excellence.
It has been accredited by the Ministry of Health out of 38 qualified hospitals for medical tourism and medical treatment in Turkey
